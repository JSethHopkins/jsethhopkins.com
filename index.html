The Practice

Consulting at Forvis Mazars

As Director of Consulting at Forvis Mazars, I work directly with C‑suite executives and boards on the problems that don't fit neatly inside one department — the ones that touch people, money, and technology all at once.

That has meant designing forecasting models and board-level reporting frameworks, advising audit committees on governance and risk, launching the firm's Chicago nonprofit advisory practice, and stepping in as interim CFO and COO for organizations navigating transition. The through-line is operational clarity: helping leadership see how their organization actually runs, then making deliberate decisions about how it should.
